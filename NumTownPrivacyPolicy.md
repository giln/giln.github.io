Privacy Policy

NumTown is an educational app that helps kids learn multiplication tables.

The app uses the microphone and speech recognition to recognize spoken answers during gameplay. Audio is processed only for this purpose. Recordings are not stored, shared, or kept after recognition.

We do not collect personal data from users.

We do not share data with third parties.

We do not collect location data.

We do not use advertising trackers or allow advertising companies to collect data through the app.
