Age Suitability Statement

NumTown is designed for children aged 6 to 8.

The app helps children practice multiplication tables through short educational gameplay.

The content is focused on arithmetic practice and does not include social networking, user-generated public content, chat, advertising, or links to external websites intended for children.

The app uses the microphone and speech recognition to recognize spoken answers during gameplay. Audio is processed only for this purpose. Recordings are not stored, shared, or kept after recognition.

The app does not collect personal data from children.

The app may offer subscriptions. Subscription purchase flows are placed behind a parental gate so that children cannot access them directly without adult involvement.

The app does not use advertising trackers or allow advertising companies to collect data through the app.
